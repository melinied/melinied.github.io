About Melinda

"I always wonder why birds stay in the same place when they can fly anywhere on the earth. Then I ask myself the same question."

Born in Honkong and raised in Sweden, Melinda was always the creative one amongst her friends and family. She was always seen during high school with her nose in a book

She studied computer science in Malmö Högskola and took classes in Business & Economics. The plan was to be able to start and maintain her own business.

After graduating she has fond an interest in Graphic Design & Web design. Which is what brings you to this website
